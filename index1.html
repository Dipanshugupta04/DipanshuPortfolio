ML-Integrated Smart Healthcare System

This diagram illustrates the flow of our smart healthcare platform that combines appointment scheduling with ML-powered symptom analysis and health recommendations.

Patient Interaction

Patient enters symptoms, medical history, and preferences through the platform interface or chatbot.

System Processing

System collects and organizes patient data, checks doctor availability, and prepares for ML analysis.

ML Analysis

Machine Learning models analyze symptoms, provide preliminary diagnosis, and suggest appropriate doctors.

Appointment Scheduling

System suggests available time slots based on patient preferences and doctor availability.

Doctor Consultation

Doctor reviews ML suggestions, confirms diagnosis, provides treatment plan, and prescribes medication.

Chatbot Support

AI-powered chatbot provides 24/7 support, medication reminders, and follow-up care instructions.

Health Outcomes

Personalized health recommendations, continuous monitoring, and improved patient outcomes.

Data Collection

System collects feedback and outcomes to continuously improve ML models and healthcare recommendations.
